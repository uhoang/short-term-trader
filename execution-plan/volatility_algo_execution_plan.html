03
Signal Engine — 4 Strategy Implementations
Weeks 5–7 8 tasks ~45 hrs
CORE BUILD
⌄
Week 5 · Days 1–3 · 2-WEEK TRADE
Strategy 1: Catalyst Capture Scanner
  • Signal: IV Rank >70 + event within 10 trading days + ATR ratio >1.5
  • Entry: T−1 before confirmed catalyst date, limit order at prior close +0.5%
  • Exit: T+8 to T+12 trading days OR −7% hard stop OR +15% take profit
  • Position size: Kelly fraction with 50% cap, max 5% NAV per trade
  • Sector filter: semis prioritized (export control expertise edge)
Week 5 · Days 3–5 · 2-WEEK TRADE
Strategy 2: Volatility Breakout
  • Signal: BB width at 6-month low + ATR(5)/ATR(20) > 1.3 + volume spike >150% avg
  • Direction: long if price breaks above upper band, short if below lower band
  • Entry: breakout candle close + confirmation next bar open
  • Exit: opposite band touch OR 10 trading days OR −6% stop
  • Filter: only trade when sector ETF is in same direction (trend alignment)
Week 6 · Days 1–3 · 1-MONTH TRADE
Strategy 3: Mean Reversion
  • Signal: VWAP deviation < −2σ + RSI(14) < 32 + sector ETF RSI > 45
  • Entry: close on signal day OR next open with limit at −0.3% from prior close
  • Exit: return to VWAP (0 deviation) OR +22 trading days OR −8% stop
  • Long-only variant first; add short leg in Phase 4 after backtest validation
  • Cyber and software sectors: highest mean reversion alpha historically
Week 6 · Days 3–5 · 3-MONTH TRADE
Strategy 4: Sector Momentum / Pairs
  • Rank stocks within sector by vol-adj momentum score weekly
  • Long top quintile (3 stocks), short bottom quintile (3 stocks) per sector
  • Rebalance every 15 trading days; exit if rank drops below median
  • Pairs variant: Z-score spread between correlated names (e.g. NVDA/AMD) > 2σ
  • Market-neutral construction: dollar-matched long/short per sector
Week 7 · Days 1–2
Signal Aggregation & Conflict Resolution
  • Build unified signal bus: each strategy emits SignalEvent(ticker, direction, strength, strategy_id)
  • Conflict rule: if Strategy 1 long and Strategy 3 short same ticker, suppress both
  • Signal strength aggregation: weighted average when same direction, different strategies
  • Max concurrent positions: 15 open trades across all strategies
Week 7 · Days 2–4
Risk Filter Module
  • Pre-trade VaR check: reject if new trade pushes portfolio 1-day VaR > 3% NAV
  • Sector concentration cap: no single sector > 40% of open exposure
  • Drawdown kill switch: halt all new entries if portfolio DD > −10% from peak
  • Liquidity check: reject if 1-day ADV < 10× position size at entry
  • Correlation check: reject if new position >0.85 corr with existing position
Week 7 · Day 5
Integration Tests — Full Signal Pipeline
  • End-to-end test: inject synthetic OHLCV → assert correct signals fire on known setups
  • Stress test: run pipeline on March 2020 data — verify kill switch triggers, no crashes
  • Throughput test: full 60-ticker daily scan completes in <30 seconds
  • Golden file tests: specific historical dates produce deterministic signal outputs

04
Backtesting Engine & Walk-Forward Validation
Weeks 8–10 6 tasks ~40 hrs
VALIDATION GATE
⌄
Week 8 · Days 1–3
vectorbt Backtest Engine Setup
  • Install vectorbt with numba acceleration; validate GPU mode if CUDA available
  • Custom Portfolio class with per-trade log, slippage model (0.05%), commission ($0.005/sh)
  • Realistic fill simulation: limit orders use next-bar open, market orders use VWAP estimate
  • Short-selling support: borrow cost 0.5%/yr default, configurable per ticker
  • Multi-strategy portfolio aggregation: combine all 4 strategies into single portfolio view
Week 8 · Days 3–5
Walk-Forward Framework
  • Implement rolling window: 18-month in-sample, 6-month OOS, step 3 months
  • Parameter optimization within each in-sample window (grid search)
  • Strict: optimized params never applied retroactively to prior OOS windows
  • Regime tagging: label each period (risk-on, risk-off, high VIX >25, low VIX <15)
  • Store results per window: Sharpe, max DD, win rate, sector attribution
Week 9 · Days 1–3
Per-Strategy Backtest Runs
  • Run each strategy standalone 2018–2025, full universe, all sectors
  • Strategy 1 (catalyst): target Sharpe >1.2, win rate >52%
  • Strategy 2 (breakout): target Sharpe >0.9, max DD <12%
  • Strategy 3 (mean rev): target Sharpe >1.5, avg hold 14–18 days
  • Strategy 4 (momentum/pairs): target Sharpe >1.8, market-neutral beta <0.15
Week 9 · Days 3–5
Statistical Significance & Bias Testing
  • Bootstrap permutation test: shuffle signal dates 1000×, compare to real Sharpe
  • t-test: assert mean trade return significantly > 0 (p < 0.05) per strategy
  • Survivorship bias check: re-run with delisted tickers included
  • Look-ahead audit: automated check that no feature uses future data
  • Transaction cost sensitivity: re-run at 2× and 5× baseline costs
Week 10 · Days 1–3
Combined Portfolio Backtest
  • Run all 4 strategies simultaneously with risk filter active
  • Correlation analysis between strategy returns (target <0.5 pairwise)
  • Portfolio-level Sharpe, Sortino, Calmar ratios
  • Drawdown analysis: duration, recovery time, worst 5 drawdown periods
  • Sector attribution: which sectors generate most alpha per strategy
Week 10 · Days 3–5
Backtest Performance Dashboard
  • pyfolio full tearsheet: equity curve, rolling Sharpe, drawdown chart
  • Per-strategy comparison table: Sharpe, DD, win rate, avg hold
  • Sector heatmap: alpha contribution by sector × strategy matrix
  • Regime performance breakdown: high vs low VIX, bull vs bear periods
  • Export PDF report for each strategy + combined portfolio

05
Optimization, Paper Trading & Refinement
Weeks 11–13 5 tasks ~35 hrs
TUNING
⌄
Week 11 · Days 1–4
Hyperparameter Optimization
  • Optuna Bayesian search on key parameters: lookback windows, thresholds, stop levels
  • Objective: maximize OOS Sharpe, constrain max DD < 12%
  • 3-fold walk-forward CV inside optimization loop (no data leakage)
  • Sensitivity analysis: plot Sharpe vs each parameter in isolation
  • Select robust parameters (performance flat across ±20% of optimal value)
Week 11 · Day 5 – Week 12 · Day 2
Regime Detection & Adaptive Signals
  • Markov regime detection on VIX + sector vol (Hidden Markov Model, 2 states)
  • High-vol regime (>VIX 22): disable momentum, amplify mean reversion
  • Low-vol regime (<VIX 15): boost catalyst capture allocation
  • Energy sector: add crude oil price trend filter (WTI 20d vs 60d MA)
  • Backtest with regime-switching vs static — assert improvement in Sharpe
Week 12 · Days 2–4
Paper Trading Launch (Alpaca)
  • Deploy signal scanner as daily cron (runs at 3:45 PM ET, sends pre-market alerts)
  • Connect to Alpaca paper account: auto-submit limit orders at 9:32 AM ET open
  • Live position tracker: open trades, unrealized P&L, days held
  • Manual override interface: CLI command to cancel/override any pending order
  • Daily Telegram summary: new signals, filled orders, closed trades, running P&L
Week 12 · Day 5 – Week 13 · Day 3
Paper Trade Monitoring & Calibration
  • Run 3 weeks of paper trading — log every fill, slippage vs backtest assumption
  • Compare live signal timing to backtest: identify systematic timing errors
  • Slippage calibration: adjust backtest fill assumptions to match live observations
  • False positive audit: manually review every signal that hit stop loss
  • Win rate and hold duration vs backtest expectation — flag >10% deviation
Week 13 · Days 3–5
Dashboard & Reporting System
  • Streamlit dashboard: live signals, open positions, equity curve, sector breakdown
  • Backtest vs live comparison panel: Sharpe, win rate, avg return side by side
  • Weekly PDF report: auto-generated every Sunday, emailed to self
  • Strategy health monitor: rolling 30-day Sharpe decay alert (<0.5 triggers review)
